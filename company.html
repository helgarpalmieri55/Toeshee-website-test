Our Story

Why We Were Built — And The Operational Gap We Set Out To Solve

We were built to address the operational gap in regulated digital ecosystems. As Web3 and fintech platforms scaled, customer operations became part of the risk layer — yet traditional outsourcing wasn't designed for that responsibility.

Today, Toeshee supports compliance-critical customer operations as an extension of internal teams. We bring crypto-native fluency, structured escalation models, and audit-ready processes to every engagement.
